Easy Energy solutions srl is a company founded in the summer of 2021, when a group of entrepreneurs decided to merge into a single company, in which the definition of simplicity persists at the base with a pinch of essentiality and functionality, moreover Easy also has heart the issue of the environment, so all the work is carried out in respect and protection of it.

Easy srl is like a big family, it has a team of professionals, from engineers, office workers and workers. A change has been applied for 7 months. The board appoints Marco Forcini, CEO of Tecta group, and Alessandro Sposetti as administrators.
